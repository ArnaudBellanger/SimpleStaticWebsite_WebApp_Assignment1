Transforming fire warior in vespid stingwings

This project aim to make count as vespid model. Since the basic model sold by the main producer is out of date and not very good looking so people find a way transform an existing model in vespid like model. in the photo above you can find the modifyed vespid and in the photo below the old model.

Here is a list of what is needed to make a total of 10 Vespid equivalent.

  • fire warior box
  • 10 jetpack
  • 10 infanterie gun barel
  • A bit of green Stuff
  • Modeling tools
Here is the result and as you can see the project is not finished since I need to paint my ten brand new vespids !
